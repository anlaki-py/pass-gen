To My Love, Rui

Dear Rui,

As I sit down to pen this letter, my heart is filled with so many emotions that I barely know where to begin. But I do know that I must express my love and gratitude for the beautiful, extraordinary person that you are. You have brought so much joy, laughter, and warmth into my life, and I cannot imagine a world without you in it.

From the moment our paths crossed, I knew that something special was happening. There was an undeniable spark between us, a connection so strong that it felt like fate had brought us together. Every moment we spent together was filled with joy, and I could not help but feel that I had found the person I was meant to share my life with.

Rui, you have the most incredible heart, filled with kindness, empathy, and a deep sense of compassion. Your unwavering support and understanding have meant the world to me, and I am so grateful for the love and care you have shown me. You have taught me to be a better person, and for that, I am eternally thankful.

Your laughter is infectious, and the way you light up a room is truly a sight to behold. Your passion for life and desire to make the world a better place is inspiring, and it has made me a better person. I am so lucky to have you as my partner, and I promise to love and cherish you for all the days of my life.

I love the way we can sit in silence together, just enjoying each other's presence, and how we can have deep, meaningful conversations that last for hours. I love how we can make each other laugh until our sides hurt, and how we can comfort each other during difficult times. I love how we can be ourselves around each other, without fear of judgment or rejection.

Rui, you are my soulmate, my best friend, and the love of my life. I am so proud to call you mine, and I cannot wait to see what the future holds for us. I promise to always be by your side, supporting you, and loving you unconditionally, just as you do for me.

Thank you for choosing me, for allowing me to be a part of your incredible journey. I am so grateful for the love we share, and I look forward to a lifetime of happiness and adventure with you.

❤️

With all my love,

Aki
