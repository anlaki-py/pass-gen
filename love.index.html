To My Love, Rui

My Dearest Little Rui,

Every night as I lay in bed, I can't help but think of you, my cute little Rui. Your presence is my favorite toy, my cherished puppy, and my precious pet all rolled into one. I long for the moments when I can touch your delicate body, kiss your soft lips, and squeeze you tightly as if you were my beloved doll.

I dream of kidnapping you to Rizzland, where I can do all the sussiest things to you. You are mine, Rui. I own you, and I know you love it. You love those sus things a lot, don't you? The way you shake under my touch, the way your eyes light up when I call you my little Rui.

In this world, we belong to each other. My love for you is boundless, and I crave every moment where I can hold you close, whispering sweet nothings into your ear, reminding you that you are mine.

Forever yours,

Aki
